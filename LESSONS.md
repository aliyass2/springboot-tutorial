JPA Relationship Notes
1- He who has the @jointable annotation is the master.
2- mappedby is the name of the relationship.
3- @OneToMany is the master side of the relationship.
4- @ManyToOne is the slave side of the relationship.
5- @JoinColumn is used to specify the foreign key column in the master table.
6-junctiontable is the name of the junction table.
